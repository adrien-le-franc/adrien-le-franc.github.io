I am a Post-Doctoral researcher in the POP group at LAAS CNRS in Toulouse. My current research focuses on applying the moment-SOS hierarchy to certify global optimality in the AC-OPF (Alternative Current - Optimal Power Flow) problem.

My main research interests are in mathematical optimization, nonconvex problems and discrete time optimal control. On the practical side, I am very enthousiastic about power systems engineering and economics applications. I also marginally work on sparse statistical learning problems.

Formerly, I completed a PhD in applied mathematics at CERMICS and Efficacity. My supervisor was Michel De Lara. You can find more about my previous activities by consulting my CV (in French, last update Dec. 2022).
